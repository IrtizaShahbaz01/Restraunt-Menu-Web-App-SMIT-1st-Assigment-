Ladoo

Ladoo is a ball-shaped sweet popular in the Indian subcontinent. It is made from various ingredients like gram flour (besan), wheat semolina (suji), and ground coconut, often mixed with sugar and ghee (clarified butter). Different types of ladoos are made during festivals and religious occasions. The texture and taste of ladoo can vary significantly depending on the ingredients used, making it a versatile and beloved sweet in Indian cuisine. Its round shape and rich, sweet flavor make it a perfect treat for celebrations.
