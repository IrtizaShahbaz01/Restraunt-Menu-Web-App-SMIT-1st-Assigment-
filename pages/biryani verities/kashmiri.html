Kashmiri Biryani

Kashmiri biryani is a traditional dish from the beautiful region of Kashmir in India. Known for its rich flavors and aromatic spices, this biryani is a true delight for the senses. The dish typically features a mix of marinated meat, fragrant basmati rice, and a blend of spices like saffron, cardamom, and cinnamon. The addition of dried fruits like raisins and apricots adds a touch of sweetness to the dish, creating a unique and delicious flavor profile. Kashmiri biryani is a must-try for those looking to experience the rich culinary heritage of Kashmir.
