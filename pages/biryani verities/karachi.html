Karachi Biryani

Karachi biryani is a popular variation of the classic biryani dish, originating from the vibrant city of Karachi in Pakistan. Known for its bold flavors and spicy kick, this biryani is a favorite among food enthusiasts. The dish typically features a mix of marinated meat, fragrant basmati rice, and a blend of aromatic spices like cumin, coriander, and red chili powder. The addition of fried onions, mint leaves, and boiled eggs adds depth and richness to the dish, creating a symphony of flavors. Karachi biryani is a must-try for those looking to experience the diverse and delicious cuisine of Pakistan.
